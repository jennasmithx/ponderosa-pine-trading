About Us

Ponderosa Pine Trading 26CC is a proud third-generation civil engineering company based in Shelly Beach, KZN. Established originally in Johannesburg under the name Dick Smith, our expertise spans decades across South Africa and beyond.

Our Journey

Since 1976, after relocating our offices to Phalaborwa, we have served regions including Venda, Gazankulu, Lebowa, Kruger National Park, Mozambique, and surrounding areas. Our long-standing presence demonstrates commitment, expertise, and reliability in every project we undertake.

Our Expertise

We specialize in civil engineering construction including retaining walls, crusher bases, columns, beams, suspended slabs, thickeners, reservoirs, concrete roads, weighbridges, silo bases, as well as residential and commercial buildings. Each project is executed with precision, safety, and quality as our guiding principles.

Our Experience

Over the past 18 years, we have successfully completed projects locally at NPC, Idwala Carbonates, and Rossmin Mine, as well as in the Western Cape for Overport Agri. Our portfolio showcases versatility and reliability across both industrial and commercial sectors.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class civil engineering solutions with integrity, quality, and efficiency, enhancing communities and industries alike.

Our Vision

To be recognized as South Africa’s leading civil engineering contractor, known for innovation, reliability, and excellence in every project.

Our Values

Integrity, Safety, Excellence, Sustainability, and Commitment — the core principles guiding all our operations.
